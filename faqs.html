FAQs

How can i place order? +

In order to place an order with khasstores , you will be required to:

  • Shop for the items you want
  • Add the items to your Shopping Cart.
  • Proceed to checkout.
  • Enter billing and shipping information( make sure the billing address is same as the shipping address).
  • Provide payment information.
  • Review and submit your order.
  • Check for order confirmation via email or text
How can I track my order? +

You can track your order by visiting the 'Track Order' page on our website. Enter your order number to get real-time updates.

How i can know that Elegance Emporium has received my order? +

After you place your order, you will receive an acknowledgement e-mail from eleganceemporium.com (info@eleganceemporium.com) to confirm that your orders have been well-received. Next working day local customers (within Pakistan) will receive a phone call from our department to confirm your order. Do note that an order will only be shipped when its credit card payment has been approved, and in case of COD orders. An order is shipped when billing and delivery address is verified..

There is missing item in my order. What should i do? +

If there is any missing item(s), please contact at us right away at the provided email: info@eleganceemporium.com or call us at: (+92) 000 000 000 during working hours.
