Return And Exchange Policy

If you’re unhappy with your purchase, we’ll find a solution for you. Most of our products can be returned or exchanged within 7 days. Note: Sale items are just exchangeable.

Exchange Policy

The product can be exchanged within 7 days after delivery.

  1. Complete the return of the order.
  2. Pack the product securely.
  3. Ship it via online departure or to our store.

Return Policy

Initiate Return:

  • Contact our customer service team at returns@eleganceemporium.com to initiate a return.
  • Provide your order number, reason for return, and whether you prefer a refund or store credit.

    • Pack the Item:

  • Pack the item securely in its original packaging.
  • Include the original receipt and any accessories, tags, or free gifts received.
  • Ship the Item:

Refund Policy

    Initiate Request

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery at refunds@eleganceemporium.com with your order number and reason for refund.

    • Prepare and Ship

  • Pack the unused item in its original packaging with tags and receipt. Ship it to our store address

    • Inspection and Processing

  • We will inspect the returned item within 3-5 business days. If approved, a refund will be issued to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

    • Receive Refund

  • A confirmation email will be sent once the refund is processed. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to our error.

Damage or Defective product

    Contact Customer Support:

  • Immediately contact our customer support team within 48 hours of receiving the damaged or defective item. Provide your order number and photos of the damage or defect.

    • Evaluation Process

  • Our team will evaluate the issue based on the provided information. If necessary, we may request additional details to process your claim.

    • Return Authorization:

  • If the item is deemed damaged or defective, you will receive a return authorization. Follow the instructions provided to return the item.

    • Replacement or Refund:

  • Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process a replacement or refund, depending on your preference and item availability.
